# Welcome to FocusPal

**ADHD-friendly screen time and activity tracker for families**

FocusPal helps children develop healthy time management habits through engaging timer experiences and visual progress tracking. Built specifically for families with multiple children, featuring intuitive timer visualizations inspired by the Time Timer concept.

## Features

- **Visual Timers** - Circular, bar, and analog timer modes
- **Multi-Child Support** - Independent profiles for each family member
- **Points & Rewards** - Gamification that genuinely motivates
- **Siri Integration** - Hands-free voice control
- **Calendar Sync** - Schedule and plan activities
- **Parent Controls** - PIN-protected settings and insights
