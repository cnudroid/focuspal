# FocusPal Marketing Strategy & Growth Plan

## Executive Summary

FocusPal is a well-designed ADHD-friendly timer and activity tracking app for families. This document outlines the strategic marketing plan to scale the app from launch to 50,000+ users within 6 months.

**Primary Positioning:**
> "The only family productivity app designed specifically for ADHD minds"

**Tagline Options:**
1. "See Time. Build Focus. Grow Together."
2. "Making Time Visible for Growing Minds"
3. "The Family Timer That Actually Works for ADHD"

## Appendix A: Competitor Analysis Detail

### Time Timer (Physical Product)
- **Price:** $25-40
- **Pros:** Established brand, tactile, no screen
- **Cons:** Single purpose, no tracking, no multi-child
- **Our positioning:** "All the benefits of Time Timer, plus tracking, gamification, and insights"

### Forest App
- **Price:** $3.99 one-time
- **Pros:** Beautiful design, gamification
- **Cons:** Not designed for kids/ADHD, no parent features
- **Our positioning:** "Built specifically for ADHD minds, not just focus enthusiasts"

### OurHome App
- **Price:** Free with premium
- **Pros:** Family chore management
- **Cons:** No ADHD focus, no visual timers
- **Our positioning:** "Purpose-built for ADHD families, not just chore tracking"

## Appendix B: Brand Guidelines Summary

### Voice & Tone
- **Empathetic:** We understand the daily struggles
- **Empowering:** Focus on capability, not disability
- **Practical:** Actionable advice, not just sympathy
- **Celebratory:** Every win matters

### Messaging Do's
- "Makes time visible"
- "Built for ADHD minds"
- "Grow together as a family"
- "See progress, not just problems"

### Messaging Don'ts
- Never use "fix" or "cure" language
- Avoid deficit framing
- Don't promise miracles
- Never shame parents or children

### Visual Identity
- Bright, engaging colors
- Clean, uncluttered layouts
- Child-friendly but not childish
- Accessible contrast ratios
